Willow's Parrot sidekick (WillowSidekick.Parrot) loads its model from THIS folder:
    Resources.Load<GameObject>("Models/Sidekicks/Raven_Parrot/Raven_Parrot")

This one is RIGGED with 5 baked clips, so it needs an AnimatorController.
Full steps: docs/ASSET_IMPORT_Meshy.md  (section: Willow's Parrot)

Needed in this folder:
    Raven_Parrot.prefab          <- model with Animator + Raven_Parrot.controller assigned
    Raven_Parrot.controller      <- references the 5 baked clips (Walking is the looping default)
    the 5 *_withSkin.fbx files    (mesh + one clip each; set Rig = Generic)
    *_texture_0.png               (base colour)
    *_texture_0_normal.png        (set Texture Type = Normal map)

The raw ~167 MB Meshy export is intentionally NOT in the scaffold zip — keep it local.
Until Raven_Parrot.prefab exists, the sidekick falls back to its tinted placeholder (safe).
Delete this marker once the prefab is in.
