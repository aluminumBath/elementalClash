## Deliverable standard per finished 3D asset

```text
asset_slug/
  source/
    original_png.png
    turnaround_front.png
    turnaround_side.png
    turnaround_back.png
  blender/
    asset_slug_clean.blend
  unity/
    asset_slug.glb or asset_slug.fbx
    textures/
    preview.png
  notes.md
```

## Acceptance checklist

- Looks good from front, side, and back.
- Matches concept art silhouette.
- Uses medieval robes/casual attire for non-channelers.
- Has no copied franchise symbols or costume shapes.
- Mesh origin is at feet/base.
- Scale is correct in Unity.
- Materials are named and organized.
- Colliders are hand-created, not auto-generated.
- Rig works if animated.
